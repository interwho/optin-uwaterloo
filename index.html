Current-status

We're going to go with the petition and student council approach. A few of us are meeting with the Feds President monday to work out the Feds-logistics, and we'll have a platform site up by next Friday.

A few of us will also be running for and calling a by-election of student council after the official Feds elections so we can get more representation there. We'll all be running under the same platform; to call the referendum. Interested in running with us? Let me know.

We'll also be registering under an official feds club, called Opt-In UWaterloo.

The referendum we hope to call will be about making the WPIRG fee opt-in (you choose to pay the fee vs you choose to get a refund) instead of opt-out.
