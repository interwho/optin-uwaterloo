Opt-In Uwaterloo

 

Hi,

We are Opt-In UWaterloo, a group on campus pushing for a referendum to make the WPIRG fee opt-in instead of opt-out.

 

Waterloo Public Research Group (WPRIG) levies a $4.75 per term on each undergraduate student attending the University of Waterloo. This fee, although optional, unfortunately has a lengthy opt-out process. In essence, if you want to receive a refund, you have to take time out during your busy school schedule (between 11am – 4pm) and walk all the way to their offices in Conrad Grebel University College.

 

This is an enormous inconvenience to students and as a result many students are either unaware of the fee or simply pass it off as a cost of attending university here in Waterloo.

 

WPRIG, an organization which should maintain a neutral opinion, has taken on sides in quite a few controversial conflicts. These biased views have caused a division among the student population.

 

Additionally, Here are some other controversial points about WPRIG:

  • 60% of budget goes towards salaries
  • Students voted no for their referendum
  • Overspent on their campaigns
  • Utilized questionable campaigning tactics
  • FEDS supported the initial funding scheme back in the 80s
  • Provides redundant services

In conclusion, it is clear that WPRIG does not represent the general interest of all students, as a result the fee levied on students should not be automatically charged.

We emphasize, we're not trying to shut down WPIRG. We heavily emphasize that whoever supports what WPIRG does still has the option to opt in

So, Join us in making the WPRIG fee Opt-In.
